About Alyssa

Alyssa is twenty eight years old and lives in a beautiful Southern California beach town, with her husband and two dogs. She studied at the California State University of Channel Islands, where she received her Bachelor’s in Clinical Psychology. Her family and friends are most important to her and, if she doesn’t have a book in hand or isn’t writing one of her stories, you can find her walking on the beach with her husband or enjoying a beautiful hike in the mountains.
